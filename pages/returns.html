Returns & Refunds

Easy returns within 30 days of purchase

How to Return

Step 1: Initiate Return

Log into your account and select the items you wish to return from your order history.

Step 2: Select Reason

Choose the reason for your return and follow the instructions to print your return label.

Step 3: Ship Items

Pack the items securely and drop off at your nearest shipping location.

Step 4: Get Refund

Once we receive and process your return, we'll issue your refund within 5-7 business days.

Return Policy

Eligible Items

  • Unworn items with original tags attached
  • Items in original packaging
  • Items purchased within last 30 days

Non-Returnable Items

  • Worn or damaged items
  • Intimate apparel and swimwear
  • Sale items marked as final sale
  • Gift cards

Refund Options

  • Original payment method
  • Store credit
  • Gift card

Processing Time

  • 3-5 days for return processing
  • 5-7 days for refund to appear
  • Instant store credit option

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I have to return an item?

You have 30 days from the delivery date to return most items.

Is return shipping free?

Yes, we provide a free return shipping label for all domestic returns.

How will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days after we receive your return.
