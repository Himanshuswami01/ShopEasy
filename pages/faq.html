Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

What are your shipping options?

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping (3-5 business days)
  • Express Shipping (1-2 business days)
  • International Shipping (7-14 business days)

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be unworn and in original condition with tags attached.

How long do refunds take to process?

Refunds typically process within 5-7 business days after we receive your return.

How do I find my size?

Check our size guide for detailed measurements. Each product page also includes specific sizing information.

Are your products sustainable?

We are committed to sustainability and use eco-friendly materials whenever possible. Look for our "Eco-Friendly" badge on products.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

How do I reset my password?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Which countries do you ship to?

We ship to over 100 countries worldwide. Check our shipping page for the complete list and delivery times.

Do I have to pay customs fees?

Customs fees vary by country and are the responsibility of the customer. Please check your local customs regulations.

How should I wash my garments?

Always check the care label on each garment. Most items can be machine washed cold, but some require special care or dry cleaning.

How can I maintain the quality of my clothes?

To maintain quality:

  • Follow care label instructions
  • Wash similar colors together
  • Use gentle detergents
  • Avoid excessive heat when drying
  • Store items properly
